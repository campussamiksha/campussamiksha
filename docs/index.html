Every institution scored on what actually matters to its people.

Teaching faculty, non-teaching staff and research scholars each rate the parameters relevant to their role — from salary punctuality and academic freedom to stipend adequacy and guide quality — aggregated into one honest picture.

What it does

Reviews

Role-aware ratings

Faculty, staff and scholars answer the parameters that fit their role — no one-size-fits-all form.

Trust

Anonymous & verified

Identity is never shown to employers. Upload proof once for a “Verified employee” badge — the document is deleted after review.

Pay

Salary explorer

Structured, anonymous pay data with medians and a salary-punctuality signal — the thing candidates most want to know.

Hiring

Interview experiences

What the process and questions were really like, so the next candidate walks in prepared.

Fairness

Claim & right of reply

Institutions can verify their profile and post one official response per review — reply, never edit or hide.

Integrity

Moderation & anti-defamation

Every submission is reviewed; content names roles, not individuals. Report & takedown built in.

How it works

Step 01

Share your experience

Sign up, verify your email, and review an institution you worked at — pros, cons, and role-specific ratings.

Step 02

A moderator checks it

Submissions are held until reviewed for authenticity and to keep them fair and lawful.

Step 03

It goes live

Approved reviews publish and recalculate the institution's scores — helping the next person decide.

A national directory, ready on day one

Seeded with 13,000+ AICTE-approved institutions across every state and union territory — searchable by name, acronym, city or state.
